FrozenPlain

FrozenPlain is my main work. It's a company that makes sample-based software synthesisers and audio plugins; they're tools for composers & producers to make music.

Signet

Signet is a free open-source command-line tool for bulk editing audio files. It's very useful for sample-library developers who often work with 100s of audio files that need to processed in a similar fashion so they can be effectively used in a sampler.
